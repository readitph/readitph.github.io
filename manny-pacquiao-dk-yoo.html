Promoter Hinihimok si Pacquiao Na Lumaban Sa Totoong Boksingero o si McGregor kaysa kay Yoo

Posted on August 2, 2022

Pinayuhan ng international boxing promoter na si Aljoe Jaro ang boxing icon at dating Senador Manny Pacquiao na huwag ituloy ang kanyang laban sa South Korean YouTuber na si DK Yoo, na naka schedule sa buwan ng Desyembre at magaganap sa Seoul, South Korea.

Ayon kay Aloe Jaro na isang Filipino boxing promoter na naka base sa United States, para mas kumita ay dapat mas piliin ni Pacquiao na lumaban sa totoong boksingero o sa isang popular na mixed martial artist kagaya nalang ni Conor McGregor, kahit ano pa man ang uri ng laban nila ni Yoo.

"He shouldn’t fight that Korean first of all. If he really wants to come back and fight, he should be fighting a real boxer or any popular fighter who has a name like McGregor that can entertain millions of fans around the world," sabi ni Jaro.

“Those type of fights are killing the sport of boxing. If they want some entertainment, they must choose a better fighter than that — like a real boxer who already retired with the same weight class. At the same time, nobody knows who’s that Korean,” dagdag pa niya.

Si Yoo ay isang fighting coach na mayroong 654, 000 na subscriber sa YouTube at founder ng Warfare Combat System. Pero ayon kay Aljoe Jaro walang nakakakilala sa Korean YouTuber sa US kahit na sinabi ni Yoo na may plano siyang magkaroon ng exhibition fight kay McGregor, Floyd Mayweather at kay Logan Paul.

“Except some Koreans, nobody knows him especially in the US. The weight and the height difference is not good too. That Korean is taller and I think heavier than him. I expect that fight may end up to a wrestling bout,” sabi ni Jaro.

Ang isang regular na boxing o kahit exhibition man laban sa isang sikat na fighter, ayon kay Jaro, ay makakapagbigay kay Pacquiao ng malaking kita para masupoetahan ang kanyang layunin, ang mabigyan ng kanlingan ang mga wala tirahan.

“If he wants to fight for charity to help more Filipinos, I think he should choose a well-known attractive rival that can get him millions of earnings from the fight,” paliwanag ni Jaro. “He is Manny Pacquiao and respected by the whole world. He has a big name in the boxing world.”
